About UsMeet Our CEO

Sharon C. Jordan is the Founder and CEO of Speech Tactics  with 8+ years of  executive leadership experience and 10+  years of clinical experience. Sharon formed Speech Tactics in 2012 with the intent of serving a just small number of private practice clients outside of her full-time hospital position as a medical speech language pathologist.  Within the span of a year, Sharon‚Äôs private practice caseload gradually increased  as she became more convinced of her true passion and purpose for working with children. By 2014, Sharon was juggling two jobs as her pediatric clientele grew steadily. Sharon struggled daily with the decision to leave the hospital position, but eventually she relented and went on to pursue what she now knows is her purpose.  

Since 2014, Sharon has led the Speech Tactics organization through several growth milestones, starting from 200 ft.¬≤ and growing to 6000 ft.¬≤  of  clinic space. As a ‚Äúpeople-first‚Äù leader, Sharon spends a great deal of time building relationships with her team and with the families at Speech Tactics. Through the design, development, and implementation of organizational systems, operations, and programs, she has created a culture and environment that focuses on how clients and employees experience Speech Tactics. At the core of her mission, Sharon aims to ensure everyone who walks through the doors at Speech Tactics has a positive experience, learn something, and leaves with a smile on their face. 

Sharon is a collaborative and hands-on leader who encourages creativity, communication, exploration, and courage with her team.  She is solution-oriented and is constantly seeking new ways to grow,  rethink, and transform pediatric therapy. Many people have described Sharon as an intuitive thought leader whose visionary gifts have propelled Speech Tactics to new heights in pediatric therapy. When Sharon is not leading her dynamic and diverse team of AMAZING therapists, she enjoys spending quality time with her family and friends. She loves reading books about history, human behavior, and personal growth.  She embraces the journey of leadership and shows up each day ready to face new challenges that will ascend the Speech Tactics organization to higher levels of excellence!

Comprehensive Services at Speech Tactics

We’re In This Together Together!

Speech Tactics, since 2012, has been committed to implementing effective, research-based therapeutic interventions to make sure our clients reach their optimal potential and achieve their functional therapy goals. Our team of dedicated professionals works closely together to collaborate, develop, and implement individualized therapy plans for our clients and families. It is our philosophy that every family and child we serve deserves to be treated with compassion and viewed with open-mindedness. This enables us to employ a holistic approach to effectively treat our clients’ specific needs in the areas of speech, language, feeding/swallowing, cognition, and many other activities of daily living. The most important players on our team are our wonderful parents, whom we rely on to carry over strategies and techniques in the home setting to ensure our clients receive ongoing practice and application in their natural environments outside of our clinic. Parents are a child’s greatest advocate, and our team at Speech Tactics is here to help support and strengthen that advocacy.  Remember… We’re all in this together!
